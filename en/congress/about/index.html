We cordially invite

to publish the papers of the

VIIth Congress of the International Society for Dialectology and Geolinguistics (SIDG) in Vienna and to celebrate the centenary of the Commission for the Creation of the Dictionary of Bavarian Dialects in Austria (WBÖ)!

In the course of an international event titled with dialect 2.0 and wboe100 we appreciated interdisciplinary exchange of ideas, the presentation of current research results and visions focussing dialectology, geolinguistics and lexicography and making new collaborations and - most of all - friends.

Thank you!
