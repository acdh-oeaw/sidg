We are looking forward to new faces!

For this reason we offer special conditions and free admission to the congress for students:

Reduced admission see under: Admission

Free admission for voluntary service:

Students will have the opportunity to participate in the congress actively.
For voluntary service you will be part of the congress organisation in particular areas: communication with participants, website updating, publication of proceedings, handling of the event etc.

If interested, please send an informal application to the head of the organising team (Eveline Wandl-Vogt).
We request aspirants who are experienced in one of our subjects.

Student volunteers have the opportunity of full registration (except: congress volume only available in digital form).

Note:
Voluntary service or internships can be allowed for bachelor or diploma studies in German Philology and the study UF German at Vienna University as optional module (here you get more information to internship).

We are pleased to welcome you in our team!

Eveline Wandl-Vogt
